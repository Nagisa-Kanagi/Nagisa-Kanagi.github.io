Description

My target audience for this website is anyone living in America that may want information about the weather.

Whether they be local, or just visiting an area, it will give everyone the accurate weather information they need.

I expect that people will be trying to access my website mostly on phones and tablets, but I also need to be prepared for people accessing my site with their laptop or PC.
