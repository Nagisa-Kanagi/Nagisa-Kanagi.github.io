Description

My target audience for this website is anyone living in America that may want information or to contribute to the Salt Lake county and community.

Whether they are local, visiting the area, or they just want to contribute, it will give everyone the accurate information they need.

I expect that people will be trying to access my website mostly on laptops and PCs, but I want to be prepared for people accessing my site on mobile devices.
