Tours

Take a look at some of the popular places to visit while here on Cozumel

Cozumel Beaches

Cozumel has some of the world's most beautiful beaches, with magnificent white sand, and no short amount of coral reefs surrounding the island. Among the most popular beaches are Playa San Juan, Playa Santa Pilar, Playa San Francisco, Playa Santa Rosa, and Playa Palancar.

Chankanaab Beach Adventure Park

Discover beaches, a magnificent reef, and amusement-park-style attractions at Chankanaab Beach Adventure Park. This tropical wonderland offers walks in nature, crocodile exhibits, and sea lion shows, giving nature lovers and families a chance to get to see wildlife up close.

Dolphin Experiences

Cozumel has a number of popular dolphin experiences if you want a fun water-based activity. Some experiences include up-close encounters with these remarkable creatures, as well as use of on-site facilities such as restaurants, changing rooms, and souvenir shops.
