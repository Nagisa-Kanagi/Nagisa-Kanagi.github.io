Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

SONY
sony.com

The Sony website follows the visual hierarchy design principle very well. The elements are organized from top to bottom in a clean, linear fashion.

PARC: Proximity

Funimation
funimation.com

Funimation does a great job of keeping related elements together and seperating them into chunks that are visually appealing to the user.

White Space and Clean Design

Wizards of the Coast
company.wizards.com

Wizards of the Coast uses white space in a way where it makes their website look cleaner and well condensed where it is used.
